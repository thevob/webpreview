Our Activities

Business Publication

Our core activity is based on our annual business publication which focuses on the current trends and prospects of the business world.

BRANDrill

For the past two years, we have organized the first ever and one of the biggest national flagship branding competition from Faculty of Business Studies, University of Dhaka.

Writing Contest

We hold the only business based writing contest in Faculty of Business Studies, in order to bring out the creativity of the young minds.

Grooming Sessions

We organize grooming sessions throughout the year to prepare the students for the corporate world.

Workshops

We arrange workshops on various contemporary business related concepts to keep our young minds up to date with the business world.

Seminars

Seminars are organized by us throughout the year to make students aware of the new upcoming business trends.
