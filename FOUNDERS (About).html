Md Mahbubul Haque Khan was the dreamer of the VOB. He was the Founding Convener & Editor in Chief of The Voice of Business. Mr. Khan completed his BBA & MBA from Finance. He also completed his MSc from Leeds University, UK. Now he is PhD Research Scholar & Casual Academic, University of Newcastle, Australia; He was former Assistant Professor of Bangladesh Institute of Capital Market (BICM).

Md Mahbubul Haque Khan

Mohammed Alauddin was Head of Administration & Co-ordination Division of the organization in 2007 to 2009. At University of Dhaka, he concentrated in Management Studies. Mr. Alauddin focused his VoB leadership on revamping the culture of the organization. He is currently a Banker at private commercial BanK with experience in trade finance & investment.

Mohammed Alauddin

Md Moniruzzaman Sarker was the Editor-in-Charge (Department of Marketing, University of Dhaka) from 2007 to 2009. He was one of the editorial board members of publishing the Voice of Business magazine during this tenure. He also contributed to the publications and organizing activities of VoB. Currently he is a faculty member of a leading private university in Bangladesh.

Md Moniruzzaman Sarker

Mavin Ahmed was the founder Head of Promotion, Ideation & Events Division of the organization during 2007 to 2009. He completed his graduation and post-graduation from Dept. of Finance, University of Dhaka. He is currently volunteering as a Mentor to the Organization. Mr. Mavin is a credit risk specialist by profession with more that nine years of experience in NBFI and Credit Rating. He is currently employed with Meridian Finance & Investment Limited in Credit Risk Management Department.

Mavin Ahmed

 
Shabah kabir Nabi served as a Head of Public Relation of VOB from 2007 to 2009. At DU, she concentrated in Finance.Ms Nabi started her career at banglalink in program management dept. After that she joined at Bims as a Lecturer. In 2015 She joined Sheltech (Pvt.) Ltd. as Asst. Manager in HR department. and continue her job till now.

Shabah kabir Nabi
